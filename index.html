An Elon Timeline

1971

Elon was born in Pretoria, South Africa to engineer father and model mother.

1983

At age 12, Elon creates video game Blastar and sells it for $500.

1997

Elon graduates from Penn State with a bachelor's degrees in Economics and Physics.

1999

Elon sells Zip2, his first company with his brother, for $22 Million.
He then creates X.com, and online banking software.

2002

Sells X.com (now known as Paypal) to eBay for $1.5 billion.
He launches SpaceX soon after.

2003

Becomes a co-founder of Tesla Motors.

2006

Helped start SolarCity, a solar company to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.

2016

Founded a side hobby company, The Boring Company. This company specialized in American infrastructure and tunnel construction services.
